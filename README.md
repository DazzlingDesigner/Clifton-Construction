# Clifton Construction

Final project for Web Design II

## creator

Alexander Burkey

## Description

This website is a resource for potential clients of Clifton Construction. It provides users with information and insight into what Clifton Construction is. The layout is simple and clear so it is easy to navigate. The two main purposes of the site serves is displaying previous work and providing a way for clients to get in contact with the company.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** A friendly, community centered person that is fairly well off finantially. They have a passion for community so they keep their property maintained. With little knowledge of architecture, they look online for help with construction projects.
  
- **Secondary:** A reserved, family-oriented person. They are finantially unstable. Taking advantage of deals allow them to get by. The affordability of Clifton Construction makes it possible to repair their home.

## Project Goals

- Display the expertise and quality of work Clifton Construction does.
- Present previous projects in an organized and accessible way.
- Provide contact information to users.
- Establish a friendly identity.
